What is LabManagementSystem?

LabManagementSystem is a project to help Lab users in organizing their labs on a daily basis.

But... how?

1. First, the user is registered in this webpage and asigned the appropriate priviledged according to their position in the lab.

2. The three possible priviledges are: create, add and search.

- With "create", the users can add a new compartment to the lab (freezer or drawer), specifiyng its boxes and divisions.

- With "add", you can add a new entry to your lab's database specifiyng a reagent, its properties and location in the freezer or drawer.

- With "search", the users will be able to search for any reagent they may want, so as to know where it is located and the rest of specifications that are previously added.

3. All the users before registration has the "search" priviledge by default.

4. The rest of priviledges are added to the users when they contact with us, so as to guarantee the safety of the data.
